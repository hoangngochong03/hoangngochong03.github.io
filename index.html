Objective: Build an end-to-end pipeline that collects, cleans, enriches, and visualizes Amazon laptop data, turning unstructured product and review pages into a decision-ready Power BI dashboard.

Pipeline:

  • Enrich — classified review sentiment with a pre-trained Hugging Face model.

  • Visualize — built Power BI dashboards for price, rating, and sentiment insights.
